About

Xiuming Zhang 张修明^

I am currently working as a Research Officer at Clinical Imaging Research Center (CIRC), A*STAR-NUS, Singapore. Prior to joining CIRC, I graduated with BEng (ECE) from the National University of Singapore (NUS).

^What's in the name?
修 indicates my generation in the family tree, and 明 literally means "bright" and "wise." When put together, 修明 means "honest and enlightened (of a government)." Examples: 「水淵深廣，則龍魚生之；山林茂盛，則禽獸歸之；禮義修明，則君子怀之。故禮及身而行修，禮及國而政明。」 and 「風政修明，流愛于人，在事七年，道不拾遺。」

Research
