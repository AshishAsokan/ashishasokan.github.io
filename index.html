About

Xiuming Zhang 张修明

I am a Ph.D. candidate advised by Prof. William T. Freeman in the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL) at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). I work in the fields of computer vision, computer graphics, and computational photography, with particular interests in relighting, view synthesis, and material modeling.
