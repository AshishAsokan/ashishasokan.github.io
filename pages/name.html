Pronunciation

  • The first name 修明 (Xiuming) is pronounced approximately as "Shiew Ming."
  • The surname 张 (Zhang) is pronounced roughly as "Jang" ("J" as in "John," not "Juan").

What's in the Name?

  • 修 (Xiu) is my generation name in the family.
  • 明 (Ming) is the given name, meaning "bright" and "wise" literally.
  • When put together, 修明 (Xiuming) in ancient Chinese means "honest and enlightened (of a government)."

My Name in Chinese Classical Prose

水淵深廣，則龍魚生之；
山林茂盛，則禽獸歸之；
禮義修明，則君子怀之。
故禮及身而行修，禮及國而政明。
—— 韓詩外傳
風政修明，流愛于人，在事七年，道不拾遺。
